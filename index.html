Join a Paid Clinical Trial for Opioid Recovery

Are You Tapering Off Suboxone?

If you’re currently tapering off or recently completed a medically supervised withdrawal from Suboxone (buprenorphine), you may be eligible to participate in a paid clinical trial testing a new non-opioid treatment for opioid use disorder.

What You Receive

  • Free care and investigational medication
  • Compensation for time and travel
  • Weekly clinical support and visits

Who Can Join?

  • Ages 18–65 with moderate or severe opioid use disorder
  • Currently tapering or completed a taper within the last 3 weeks
  • Not using injection drugs more than twice weekly
  • Willing to attend weekly visits for 12 weeks
